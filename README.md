## 🐕 Pet Comedy Series

Short-form comedy videos featuring dogs. Visual-only gags — no dialogue needed. Perfect for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts.

### Episodes

| # | Title | Duration | Status | Script |
|---|-------|----------|--------|--------|
| 1 | 🔥 **Firecracker Fiasco** | ~10-15s | ✅ Script Ready | [View Script →](pet-comedy/01-firecracker-fiasco/README.md) |
| 2 | 🐱 **Cat Firecracker Fiasco** | ~15-18s | ✅ Script Ready | [View Script →](pet-comedy/02-cat-firecracker-fiasco/README.md) |

### Concept

Two dogs "team up" to light a firecracker, cover it with a bowl, and the explosion leaves one wearing the bowl as a hat. Pure visual comedy — the punchline is the dogs' reactions.

### What You Need
- 2 dogs (actors!)
- Metal dog bowl
- Firecracker (or fake prop)
- Lighter
- White bucket (for the ending gag)
- Phone camera (portrait mode)
