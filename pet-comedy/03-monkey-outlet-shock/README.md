# 🐒⚡ MONKEY + DOG POWER OUTLET — 10-Second Visual Script

> **Duration:** 10 seconds flat
>
> **Comedy Structure:** Setup → Action → Chaos → Punchline (fast, no filler)
>

## ⏱️ TIMELINE (10 SECONDS)

```
0s────────2s────────4s────────6s────────8s────────10s
|  SETUP  | ACTION |  SHOCK  | GRIN/DOG | DEADPAN END |
```

---

### SCENE 1 — "The Setup" (0:00–0:02) ⏱️ 2 sec

**On Screen:** Monkey crouching on hardwood floor, yellow screwdriver in hand, positioned near wall outlet. Rottweiler sitting beside, watching calmly.

**Camera:** Low angle, ground level, static. Portrait mode (9:16).

**Action:** Monkey holds screwdriver near outlet. Dog sits still, stoic face.

**Funny beat:** The IMPLICATION — viewer already knows what's coming.

**AI Prompt:**
```
A small capuchin monkey crouching on hardwood floor holding a yellow screwdriver near a white wall outlet. A large Rottweiler with red collar sits beside the monkey, watching calmly. Low angle, photorealistic, handheld phone camera, warm indoor lighting, portrait orientation.
```

**Sound:** Quiet suspenseful music bed (pizzicato strings)

---

### SCENE 2 — "The Insert + Shock" (0:02–0:05) ⏱️ 3 sec

**On Screen:** Monkey jams screwdriver into outlet. IMMEDIATELY — fur explodes outward, standing straight up like a sunburst. Eyes wide, mouth open in shock.

**Camera:** Same low angle. Slight digital zoom-in on the zap moment.

**Action:** Screwdriver goes in → ZAP → ALL fur stands on end. Monkey frozen in shock.

**Funny beat:** The HAIR. Absolutely ridiculous volume. Dog doesn't flinch.

**AI Prompt:**
```
A capuchin monkey on hardwood floor, ALL fur standing straight up and radiating outward like a sunburst from electrocution, eyes wide in shock, mouth open, holding a yellow screwdriver plugged into a white wall outlet. A Rottweiler dog sits calmly beside, completely unfazed, deadpan expression. Monkey hair comically puffed to maximum volume. Photorealistic, low angle, handheld phone camera, warm indoor lighting.
```

**Negative Prompt:**
```
cartoon, animated, CGI, illustration, painting, anime, 3D render, artificial, unrealistic hair, blurry, watermark, text, logo, scary, violent, blood
```

**Sound:** Metallic click → LOUD electrical ZAP + comedic "BOING"

---

### SCENE 3 — "The Grin + Dog Judges" (0:05–0:08) ⏱️ 3 sec

**On Screen:** Monkey now GRINNING — huge open-mouth smile, teeth showing, looking at camera. Fur still puffed. Dog leans in, sniffing monkey's head.

**Camera:** Same angle. Slight reframing as dog moves.

**Action:** Monkey grins maniacally. Dog snouts toward monkey's electrocuted head.

**Funny beat:** Monkey looks PROUD of itself. Dog's "bro, are you okay?" energy.

**AI Prompt:**
```
A capuchin monkey on hardwood floor with fur still puffed out, now grinning with a huge open-mouth smile showing teeth, looking at camera with manic expression, holding yellow screwdriver. A Rottweiler dog leans in close, sniffing the monkey's puffed-up head with curious expression. White wall outlet in background, warm indoor lighting. Photorealistic, low angle, handheld phone camera.
```

**Sound:** Comedic "ding" sparkle SFX + sniffing sound

---

### SCENE 4 — "The Deadpan End" (0:08–0:10) ⏱️ 2 sec

**On Screen:** Monkey stares DIRECTLY into camera. Deadpan. Completely serious. Fur slightly settling. Screwdriver at side. Dog sitting normally beside.

**Camera:** Same low angle. Static. Let the stare BURN.

**Action:** Monkey holds eye contact with camera. 2 full seconds of silence. Cut to black.

**Funny beat:** "You saw nothing. I will do it again." — fourth wall break.

**AI Prompt:**
```
A capuchin monkey sitting on hardwood floor, fur slightly messy and partially puffed, holding yellow screwdriver at its side, staring directly into camera with a completely deadpan serious expression. A Rottweiler dog sits beside, looking forward calmly. White wall outlet in background. Warm indoor lighting. Photorealistic, low angle, handheld phone camera, portrait orientation.
```

**Sound:** 1 second COMPLETE SILENCE → comedy outro sting
