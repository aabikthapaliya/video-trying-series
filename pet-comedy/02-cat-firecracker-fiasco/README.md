# 🐱🔥 CAT FIRECRACKER FIASCO — AI Video Prompt

> **Concept:** Two cats attempt a firecracker experiment. One covers it with a steel bowl, it wobbles, one cat gets curious and looks — BOOM. Black smoke face + bowl to the head. Pure comedy.
>
> **Style:** Photorealistic, cinematic, no cartoon, no AI slop
>
> **Duration:** ~15-20 seconds

## 🎬 STORY BEAT BREAKDOWN

### Beat 1 — "The Setup" (0:00–0:03)
Two cats huddle together on a concrete floor. One cat holds a firecracker (paw near it), the other watches. They look like they're plotting something mischievous. Close-up, cinematic lighting.

### Beat 2 — "Lighting It" (0:03–0:05)
The firecracker ignites — small flame and spark. One cat quickly grabs a steel bowl and slams it over the firecracker. They both hop back a step, eyes wide, watching the bowl.

### Beat 3 — "The Wobble" (0:05–0:08)
The steel bowl starts wobbling and rattling on the ground. Smoke seeps from underneath. Both cats tilt their heads in confusion, ears perked, tails flicking.

### Beat 4 — "The Brave One" (0:08–0:10)
One cat puffs its chest, acts brave, and creeps forward toward the wobbling bowl. The other cat watches from a safe distance. The brave cat leans in close, sniffing the bowl.

### Beat 5 — "The Explosion" (0:10–0:12)
BOOM. The firecracker explodes. The brave cat's face is covered in black soot and smoke. Its fur is frazzled, eyes wide in shock. It sits there stunned.

### Beat 6 — "The Laugh" (0:12–0:14)
The second cat starts laughing — mouth open, paw pointing at the soot-covered cat. Pure smug energy. It's having the time of its life.

### Beat 7 — "The Payback" (0:14–0:16)
MID-LAUGH — the steel bowl (launched by the explosion) flies through the air and bonks the laughing cat right on the head. It sits there with the bowl on its head, stunned.

### Beat 8 — "The Ending" (0:16–0:18)
Both cats: one with a soot face, one with a bowl on its head. They look at each other. Both start laughing. Freeze frame. Roll credits energy.
